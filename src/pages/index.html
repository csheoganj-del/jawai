The Legacy of Leopard Trails

Where raw granite wilderness meets uncompromising opulence.

Leopard Trails Jawai was born from a vision to redefine high-end glamping in India. Located in the majestic landscape of Jawai, Rajasthan, our resort is surrounded by dramatic billions-of-years-old rock formations, deep-rooted culture, and leopards that live in perfect harmony with the local Rabari herdsmen.

Our identity represents the marriage of micro-exclusivity with organic, lush, and avant-garde sensory detail. We offer an ultra-private sanctuary with only three hyper-luxury suites, ensuring unmatched tranquility and personalized attention for every guest.

The Safari Experience

Embark on privately guided explorations through the unique wilderness of Jawai. Track predators, explore ancient formations, and connect with heritage.

Private Leopard Tracking

Jawai's leopards have developed a peaceful co-existence with the local communities, making this the best place on Earth to observe wild leopards. Accompanied by our lead tracker, you will traverse rocky kopjes in search of these elusive big cats in absolute luxury.

We restrict our custom 4x4 safaris to a maximum of 3 vehicles in the valley at any given time, ensuring an exclusive, non-intrusive, and breathtaking experience.

Rabari Heritage Walk

Deepen your journey by walking with the Rabari pastoralists. Clad in their iconic red turbans and white linen, these ancient guardians share their lore, tracking skills, and centuries-old relationship with nature and local wildlife.

Visit local homesteads, participate in traditional chai preparation, and learn first-hand about the rich spiritual connection that protects this ecological paradise.

Granite Ridge Trekking

Explore the rugged rock formations of Jawai. These billions-of-years-old hills are rich in spiritual history, housing hidden temples inside natural caves. Our treks offer scenic views over the pristine Jawai Bandh (dam) and grasslands.

At sunset, we set up an exquisite private mobile sundowner deck atop a ridge, where you can sip champagne and enjoy custom-crafted canapés as the sky shifts to brilliant golds and magentas.
