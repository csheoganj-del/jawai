A Canvas for the Palate

Indulge in organic, wood-fired culinary masterpieces.

Dining at Leopard Trails Jawai is not just about meals; it is an immersive sensory event. Our menus are created daily based on the freshest harvests from our organic kitchen garden and local tribal farms. We specialize in reviving lost recipes of Mewari and Rajasthani royal houses, cooked slowly over hand-gathered wood embers to capture natural smoky profiles.

We do not have a standard central dining hall. Instead, every meal is served at a different, hyper-private location around the resort: on a high granite ridge at sunset, beside a crackling campfire in the scrub valley, or on your suite\'s private outpost deck.

Curated Culinary Settings

Signature Dining Outposts

The Starlight Bush Dinner

Set in an isolated scrub clearing, surrounded by natural lantern light and a large wood fire bonfire. Feast on a multi-course traditional Mewari thali containing slow-cooked amber goat curry, hand-pressed millet rotis (bajra), and organic desert berry delicacies (Ker Sangri). Listen to the distant call of night birds and the hum of the wilderness while dining under a clear Milky Way sky.

The Sunset Champagne Sundowner

Served atop a natural granite peak at dusk. A custom-built mobile bar is set up directly on the rock face, serving chilled premium champagne and botanical spirits. Enjoy a selection of warm, wood-fired appetizers such as coal-roasted sandstone corn, marinated cottage cheese skewers, and fresh local figs baked in campfire ash.

The Suite Deck Sunrise Breakfast

Wake up to the smell of fresh baking. Your butler serves a private breakfast directly on your suite deck as the sun rises over the hills. Enjoy freshly squeezed citrus juices from our orchard, house-made granola, artisanal goat cheese, and customized eggs cooked to order.

Common Questions

Dining FAQ

Can you accommodate specific dietary restrictions?

Yes, our culinary team specializes in highly personalized cuisine. Whether you require vegan, gluten-free, keto, lactose-free, or specific organic-only diets, our chef will coordinate with you prior to arrival to design a custom menu.

Are alcoholic beverages included in the package?

All packages include a selection of premium domestic house wines, beers, and our signature botanical gin cocktails. Rare international spirits, aged single malts, and select champagne labels can be ordered from our cellar menu.

Do you allow dining settings for private celebrations?

Yes, we love organizing bespoke settings for honeymoons, anniversaries, or private birthdays. We can arrange live folk musicians, custom fire-baskets, and tailor-made menus.
