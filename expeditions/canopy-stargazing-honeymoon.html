Bespoke Outpost Case Study

Honeymoon at the Obsidian Canopy

Detailing a 4-day customized honeymoon itinerary focusing on high-end wilderness comfort, sunset sundowners, private astronomy stargazing, and guest feedback.

✦ Guest Desire & Challenge

A newlywed couple from Mumbai booked our top-tier suite, the Obsidian Canopy Tent, for a 3-night honeymoon. They wanted a perfect balance of wilderness adventure and deep relaxation, requesting complete privacy, customized gourmet settings, and a special night stargazing session to celebrate their wedding. The challenge was maintaining absolute outpost seclusion while coordinating multiple customized safaris and dining setups.

✦ Scout & Field Research

Our resort concierge and astronomical tracker conducted pre-arrival preparations, focusing on:

  • • Celestial Mapping: Checked astronomical charts to identify peak transit times for Jupiter and Saturn during their stay.
  • • Dining Locations: Inspected private clearings in the scrub valley to set up a secluded, candle-lit dinner.
  • • Suite Prep: Coordinated the butler team to customize amenities, including vintage copper tub setups and local Rajasthani wildflower decorations.

✦ Tailoring the Itinerary

We developed a customized honeymoon program:

  • • Night 1: Private starlight bush barbecue set up in a secluded forest clearing.
  • • Night 2: Astronomical stargazing session on their suite\'s two-tiered deck, utilizing a computerized Meade telescope with a guide.
  • • Day 3: Late afternoon private leopard safari ending at a scenic granite ridge sunset Sundowner.
  • • Night 3: Rose-petal copper soaking tub setup under the open sky, accompanied by a bottle of chilled vintage champagne.

✦ Equipment & Outpost Assets Used

The Obsidian Canopy features premium assets tailored for luxury relaxation:

  • • Astronomy Setup: A Meade LX90 8-inch Schmidt-Cassegrain computerized telescope for clear lunar and planetary viewing.
  • • Honeymoon Bath Setup: A double-walled vintage copper soaking tub on the private deck, complete with organic essential oils and locally harvested wildflower salts.
  • • Private Sundowner Deck: Custom-designed wooden deck looking out over the leopard hills.

✦ Experience Execution & Sighting Performance

The stargazing session on Night 2 was a spectacular success. The skies of Jawai are exceptionally clear due to minimal light pollution. Our astronomer guided the couple through views of Saturn\'s rings, Jupiter\'s moon alignments, and the Hercules Star Cluster. The butler team served hand-made botanical cocktails and dessert platters on the upper deck throughout the session, creating a magical, romantic atmosphere.

✦ Results & Guest Feedback

"We wanted our honeymoon to feel like a dream, and Leopard Trails delivered exactly that. Stargazing with a professional telescope on our deck while sipping cocktails, followed by a hot copper tub bath under the stars, was unforgettable. The attention to detail and privacy was absolute."

— Rohan & Shrutika S., Mumbai, India
