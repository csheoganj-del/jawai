The Philosophy of Glamping

Where avant-garde design meets untamed wilderness.

To ensure absolute privacy, peace, and micro-exclusivity, Leopard Trails Jawai limits its entire property capacity to just three hyper-luxury sanctuaries. Set along a natural granite ridge, each suite has been designed with custom-pitched heavy organic canvas walls, double-layered insulation, and polished local teak wood floors.

Our design language represents a harmonious blend of old-world safari charm and modern high-end architectural luxury. Large double-glazed glass panels form the front facade of our suites, offering uninterrupted panoramas of the ancient granite hills and starry night skies.

Common Questions

Accommodation FAQ

Are the glamping suites air-conditioned?

Yes, all three sanctuaries feature silent, climate-controlled heating and cooling systems to ensure maximum comfort throughout the year, whether during hot summers or cool winter nights.

What is included in the nightly suite rate?

The rate includes ultra-luxury accommodation, 24/7 butler service, all gourmet farm-to-table meals (breakfast, lunch, afternoon high tea, and signature bush dinners), laundry service, and custom arrival/departure transfers from nearby local stations. Safari excursions can be added as custom experiences.

Is there internet connectivity in the wilderness?

Yes, high-speed fiber Wi-Fi is available in all suites and public decks, allowing you to stay connected or work remotely while enjoying the serene views of the Rajasthani bush.
