How to Book & Plan

A seamless, personalized booking process for luxury travelers.

Unlike government-regulated tiger reserves in India which require booking permits months in advance through rigid online lottery portals, Jawai offers a flexible, private reserve booking system. The wilderness areas are owned and managed by the local village panchayats and communities, allowing for a more personalized safari structure.

However, because Leopard Trails maintains only three private suites, accommodation and jeep slots are highly restricted. We recommend planning your journey at least 30 to 60 days in advance, especially during the high peak winter season (November to February).

Jawai Safari Planning Elements

1. Safari Hours & Timings

Safaris run twice daily, designed to match the peak activity hours of leopards and other wildlife:

  • ✦ Winter (Oct - Mar): Morning: 06:00 AM - 09:00 AM | Afternoon: 04:00 PM - 07:00 PM
  • ✦ Summer (Apr - Jun): Morning: 05:00 AM - 08:00 AM | Afternoon: 05:00 PM - 08:00 PM

2. What to Pack & Wearing Guidelines

Blending into the environment increases sighting success and respects wildlife boundaries:

  • ✦ Colors: Wear neutral tones (beige, khaki, sand, sage, or dark olive). Avoid bright whites, reds, or neon shades.
  • ✦ Clothing: Lightweight linen or cotton layers for afternoons. Heavy windbreaker jackets, beanies, and scarves for chilly morning drives in open jeeps.
  • ✦ Gear: High-end binoculars, sunglasses, sun hat, lip balm, and protective camera covers to block sand dust.

3. Photography Prep

Because leopards rest on high granite peaks, you will often photograph them from a distance of 50 to 150 meters. A DSLR or mirrorless camera body with a 100-400mm or 200-600mm lens is ideal. Our safari vehicles are equipped with sandbags to help support large lenses during tracking.

Common Questions

Booking FAQ

What is the cancellation policy for stay bookings?

Due to our micro capacity (only 3 suites), we apply a strict cancellation policy: bookings cancelled more than 30 days prior to arrival receive a 90% refund. Cancellations made between 15 and 30 days receive a 50% refund. Bookings cancelled within 15 days of check-in are non-refundable.

Can you arrange pickup transfers from Udaipur or Jodhpur?

Yes, our concierge can organize private luxury SUV transfers (Toyota Fortuner or Audi) from Udaipur Airport (UDR) or Jodhpur Airport (JDH). The drive takes approximately 2.5 to 3 hours through scenic highway routes.

Do you support helicopter transfers?

Yes, we have a private helipad clearance at our reserve. Helicopter charters can be organized from Udaipur, Jodhpur, or Jaipur airports directly to the outpost through our concierge desk.
